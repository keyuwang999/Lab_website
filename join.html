Why Join The Zhang Group?

Direct Mentorship

As a new group, every student receives 1-on-1 guidance from Prof. Zhang, ensuring rapid growth and solid training in scientific research.

World-Class Facilities

Access to state-of-the-art Aberration-Corrected TEMs and nanofabrication tools at SEU Wuxi Campus and partner facilities.

High Impact

Targeting top-tier journals (Nature/Science series). We work on frontier challenges in solid-liquid interfaces and HEAs.

Open Positions

We are seeking highly motivated postdocs with strong backgrounds in TEM, Materials Science, or Condensed Matter Physics.

Requirements:
  • Ph.D. in related fields (Physics, Materials, Chemistry, Microelectronics).
  • Experience in TEM characterization or in-situ techniques is preferred.
  • Strong publication record and good English writing skills.
Benefits:
  • Competitive salary package (SEU standard + Group performance bonus).
  • Opportunities for international collaboration (LBNL, UC Berkeley).
  • Support for applying for "Boyin" (Postdoc) talents program.

We recruit 1-2 Ph.D. students and 3-4 Master students every year through the School of Integrated Circuits, Southeast University (Wuxi Campus).

What we look for:
  • Curiosity driven and self-motivated.
  • Background in Physics, Material Science, Chemistry, or Electronics.
  • Programming skills (Python/Matlab) for data analysis are a plus.
2026 Intake: Please contact Prof. Zhang in advance (Summer Camp/Pre-application).

We welcome undergraduate students (from SEU or other universities) to conduct SRTP projects or graduation thesis designs in our lab.
